help

  1. Type in the box and press enter to create a new note.
  2. Double click a note to delete it.
  3. Click save to persist your notes across sessions.

Please note that your notes are not saved on a server, they are saved on your browser. If you reopen this page in a different browser, your notes will be gone.

WHY?

I find it therapeutic when I have a zillion things on my mind, to just start typing and then go through the things putting them in piles.

I'm weird, I know.
